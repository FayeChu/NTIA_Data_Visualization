FCC Data Viz - Census
All the Census datasets involved in this Capstone are provided by the US Census Bureau (https://www.census.gov) and it provides a website for people to retrieve various Census data files from - the American FactFinder (https://factfinder.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/index.xhtml). We adopted several tables in different datasets and programs as measurements. Specifically, at both state and county levels, Educational Attainment (S1501), Median Income in the Past 12 Months in 2016 Inflation-Adjusted Dollars (S1903), Total Population (B01003), Poverty Rate (S1701) are tables belonging to the American Community Survey program's 2016 ACS 5-year estimates dataset. Also, the Land Area column recorded in 2010 is inside the Population, Housing Units, Area, and Density: 2010 table, which is from the Decennial Census program's 2010 Census Summary File dataset.
